Trust Settings
1Supervised
Every action pauses for your review before executing. Nothing touches prospects without your sign-off.
2Semi-Autonomous
Low and medium-risk actions (scoring, briefing) run automatically. Cold emails to prospects still require approval.

The agent acts fully on your behalf. All actions within policy execute automatically. The audit trail always records everything.
Trust level is set via TRUST_LEVEL in your .env file. Restart the server to apply changes. This is intentional — trust is an admin-level decision, not something a rep changes on the fly.
